Hi,

I am Ivy, a student currently living in New York that wants to make the world a better place through design. I study Architecture and Communication Design at Parsons the School of Design.

Long before design school, I always knew I wanted to be a designer and innovator. I had a child in me that loves to play and explore through making. Putting aside the preconceptions set in our minds, I seize every opportunity, including problem that arises. I know I not only want to create spaces for people but to also create many other forms of design work for our world and our community.
